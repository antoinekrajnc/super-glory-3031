Super Glory 3031 is an RPG style MOBA set in a retro sci-fi future where mankind has advanced enough to join the galactic stage and elects to participate in the Undra Games. A gladiatorial event popular in the world. Super Glory 3031 aims to augment the already engaging and popular teamplay based MOBA with the fantasy and role-playing that you would expect from an RPG. Super Glory 3031 is much more than just a flavor of MOBA. We aim to make Super Glory 3031 a completely unique experience built completely from the ground up. Players can expect to customize the look of their character and tweak abilities to work more to their playstyle and strength. A ranking and leveling system will ensure that players will always have a goal to aim for and special events will offer player opportunities for exclusive and limited content.
