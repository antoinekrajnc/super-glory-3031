In the year 2908, advances in technology had virtually eliminated much of humanity’s plights. Genetically modified foods feed the world’s hungry, medical advances has all but wiped out most diseases. Earth had truly entered a golden age which we now call the Immaculate Renaissance. With nothing left to conquer on Earth, humanity set its sights on the stars. The year is now 3031, Earth’s top scientists and engineers had developed space traveling technology far enough that we could feasibly commute to distant star systems. Hundreds of thousands of sentient lifeforms were discovered and the universel truly began to understand itself. With pride being humanity’s only obstacle left to conquer, we joined the rest of the galaxy in the Undra Games. A battle arena that tests the mettle of all who dare face it. Humanity has one last challenge to face, and we’ll do it for glory.
